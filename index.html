About WOW

WOW (Wonosobo on Web) is a comprehensive Geographic Information System (GIS) platform designed to showcase the natural beauty, cultural heritage, and tourism potential of Wonosobo Regency, Central Java, Indonesia.

Interactive Mapping

Explore Wonosobo through our interactive map featuring 15 carefully selected tourist attractions with detailed information and stunning imagery.

Real-time Weather

Stay updated with current weather conditions in Wonosobo to plan your visit and outdoor activities effectively.

Statistical Insights

Access comprehensive data and statistics about Wonosobo's demographics, tourism trends, and regional information.

Modern Design

Experience a beautiful, responsive interface with dark/light mode support and smooth animations for optimal user experience.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable tourism in Wonosobo by providing accessible, accurate, and engaging information about the region's attractions, culture, and natural heritage. We aim to bridge the gap between technology and tourism, making it easier for visitors to discover and appreciate the unique beauty of Wonosobo.
